Our Mission

Pilot Elite Soccer Club will be a new and upcoming soccer organization within Surry, Stokes, and Yadkinville County. The objective is to reach out to new and upcoming players as well as teen clubs ranging from U13 to U18. As an addition, add adult leagues if possible. The primary target audience would be ages 5 to 18- and 19-year-olds. For the adult league, anyone over the age of 18 can join and play in a co-ed setting. All are welcome to sign up and play, we would love to have you join in on the fun!
